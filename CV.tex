\section{Personal Profile}

I am a PhD candidate at the \href{https://sites.google.com/view/qudyma-icmm}{QUDYMA} and \href{https://wp.icmm.csic.es/tqe/}{Q4Q} research groups at \href{https://www.icmm.csic.es/}{ICMM}-\href{https://www.csic.es/en}{CSIC},Madrid, under the supervision of \href{https://elsaprada.github.io/}{Elsa Prada} and \href{https://wp.icmm.csic.es/tqe/people/ramon-aguado/}{Ramón Aguado}. My research focuses on the theoretical study of topological superconductors with hybrid materials, with special emphasis on full-shell nanowires. I am particularly interested in the topological properties of superconducting materials and their potential applications in quantum computing.
